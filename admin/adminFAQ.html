ADMIN FAQ PAGE

ADD SERIES

To add a series follow these steps.
1. All fields must be filled in to add a series
2. Enter the series number you would like to add in the box.
3. Click the 'Submit' button once finished.

EDIT SERIES

To edit a series follow these steps.
1. Enter the series number you would like to edit.
2. Enter the new series number you would like to change the series too. (This will change all existing cards of the series to the new series)
3. Click the 'Submit' button once finished.

DELETE SERIES

To delete a series follow these steps.
1. All fields must be filled in to delete a series.
2. Enter the series number you would like to delete.
3. Click the 'Submit' button once finished.

ADD CARD

To add a card to a series follow these steps.
1. All fields must be filled in to add a card to an existing series.
2. Enter the series number you would like to add the card too.
3. Enter the number of the card.
4. Upload the image of the card by clicking the 'Browse...' button.
5. Click the 'Submit' button once finished.

EDIT CARD

To edit a card follow these steps.
1. You must enter both the series number and card number of the card you would like to edit.
2. You can now change the series number, card number, card image. You can change them all or just one.
3. Once you've entered all new values or images, click on the 'Submit' button.

DELETE CARD

To delete a card follow these steps.
1. All fields must be filled in to delete a card.
2. Enter the series number of the card you would like to delete.
3. Enter the card number of the series you would like to delete.
4. Click the 'Submit' button once finished.

ADD CUSTOM ITEM

To add a custom item follow these steps.
1. All fields must be filled in to add a custom item.
2. Enter the name of the custom item.
3. Enter the size of the custom item.
4. Enter the price of the custom item.
5. Upload the image of the custom item.
6. Click the 'Submit' button once finished.

EDIT CUSTOM ITEM

To edit a custom item follow these steps.
1. All fields must be filled in to edit a custom item.
2. Enter the name of the custom item you would like to edit.
3. Enter the new name of the custom item.
4. Enter the new price of the custom item.
5. Upload a new image of the custom item.
6. Click the 'Submit' button once finished.

DELETE CUSTOM ITEM

To delete a custom item follow these steps.
1. All fields must be filled in to delete a custom item.
2. Enter the name of the custom item you would like to delete.
3. Click the 'Submit' button once finished.

VIEW CUSTOMER ORDERS

To view customer orders follow these steps.
1. Go to the Mange Custom Orders page.
2. From here you can mark orders as "APPROVED" or "SENT".
3. Additionally, you can click on the get image button for a particular order to get the image uploaded by the customer.

MANAGE EMAILS

To manage emails as well as email subsriber list follow these steps.
1. Go to the Manage Emails page.
2. From here you send emails to those subsribed to your email list.
